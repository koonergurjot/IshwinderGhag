FAQ

How does land assembly work?

We align OCP/NCP policy, identify adjacent parcels, and secure owner interest with an LOI or option. Then we package the site for developers with density, services, access, and timing. I manage owner coordination, valuation, and deal structure.

How do I sell an industrial property?

Start with a BOV. We prepare an OM, market to targeted owner‑users and investors, run tours, negotiate terms (price, dates, DD), and manage diligence through completion.

What’s the process for rezoning?

We study municipal policy/engineering, prepare a concept, and coordinate pre‑application meetings. Expect multi‑step submissions, public input, and servicing/amenity contributions. I can introduce planning consultants.

How do I start?

Email your brief or book a call. You’ll get a curated shortlist with maps and comps within 24–48 hours.
